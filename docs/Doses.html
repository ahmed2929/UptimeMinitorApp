Description:

  • change dose status

    logic

    -1 get dose by id -2 change its status to the new one

    0: Status mean it's not yet active (future dose), --default 1: Status mean it's in Transit (time is here , not yet 60 mins passed), --server action 2: status code means it's taken. , --action by user 3: it's ignored (60 minutes passed no action) --server action 4: status means its rejected
